    This project, for Daramola's class, was to find out why the invention that was given to us had an impact on the Industrial Revolution. We also had to make our own version of it and do design a special way of background knowledge such as annotated bibliographies.

    What our group (mainly the other Andres) sketched was the Singer 15ch Sewing Machine. It was one of the most first and popular sewing machines at the time.

    What our group made here was the Singer Sewing Machine out of clay and wires (to hold), we also used gold sharpies for decorations and coloring.
